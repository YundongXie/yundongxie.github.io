# Yundong Xie (谢云东)

I am a Special Research Assistant (Postdoctoral Researcher) at the School of Economics and Management, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), working with Prof. Jianping Li.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Management, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by Prof. Taizhong Hu and Prof. Qiang Wu.

My research focuses on:

- Scientometrics and Informetrics
- AI for Science
- Science and Technology Policy
- Research Evaluation
- Data-driven Intelligent Decision-making
- Computational Social Science

My recent work explores how big data and artificial intelligence can support scientific innovation evaluation, disruptive research identification, talent analytics, and journal assessment.

I have published papers in leading journals such as *Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology (JASIST)*, *Journal of Informetrics*, and *Scientometrics*. My publications mainly investigate scientific innovation, interdisciplinary research, journal evaluation, social attention, and AI-driven science analytics.

Currently, I am leading several research projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), and provincial research foundations.
